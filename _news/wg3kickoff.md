<p>
  Working group 3 had its virtual kickoff workshop on <b>12 December, 2025, from 11-14 CET</b>.
  Several people interested in actively participating in the working group were joining the discussions.
  The goal of the workshop was to get an overview of current topics and questions related to WG3.
</p>
<p>
  Below you find the program of the workshop and some files related to the meeting.
</p>

<h4>Program</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Welcome, Information on the COST Action and on WG 3, getting to know each other</li>
  <li>Scientific talks:
    <ul>
      <li>Markus Abel (Ambrosys)</li>
      <li>Boumediene Hamzi (Imperial College, Caltech)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Discussion of future working group events, wrap-up and good bye</li>
</ul>
