<p>
  Working group 3 will have its virtual kickoff workshop on <b>12 December, 2025, from 11-14 CET</b>.
  Everyone interested in actively participating in the working group is very much invited to join.
  The goal of the workshop is to get an overview of current topics and questions related to WG3.
</p>
<p>
  Below you find the program of the workshop, information on how to participate and some files related to the meeting.
</p>

<h4>Program</h4>
<ul>
  <li>Welcome, Information on the cost action and on WG 3, getting to know each other</li>
  <li>Scientific talks:
    <ul>
      <li>Markus Abel (Ambrosys)</li>
      <li>Boumediene Hamzi (Imperial College, Caltech)</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Discussion of future working group events, wrap-up and good bye</li>
</ul>

<h4>How to participate</h4>
The workshop will take place via MS Teams.
Everyone interested in the meeting can participate using this <a href="https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_MzIyNTgyODUtZGYwNS00NWNkLThhZDQtNDc1Y2E0Y2YxYzlk%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%2267610027-1ac3-49b6-8641-ccd83ce1b01f%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%2277fcd504-78b6-44c2-8bb3-2ffa4584df36%22%7d" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" class="link">Teams link</a>.
<br>
Please mute yourself by default during the meeting and use the "raise hand" functionality if you would like to join a discussion.
